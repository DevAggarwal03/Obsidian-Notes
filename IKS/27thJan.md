**Rig veda**
use: rgveda
-> earliest sacred book india
-> hymns or erses of praise
-> composed in praise of various deities
-> the Rgveda priest is known as Hotr
-> focus on composition and recitation of hymns
-> uses hymns as a means of expression and worship
-> suktas in the rgveda (curiosity and quest for new knowledge of ancient indians)
-> sukta eg: Nasadiya sukta, kiranyagarbha sukta and purusa-sukta dicuss teh origin of universe

**Yajur veda**
-> from the root "Yaj"
-> meaning worship associated with sacrifice
-> related to rituals
-> mantras in yajurveda are referred to as yajus
-> explains procedures of sacrifices
-> includes human anatomy, metals, costellation, seasons, numbers and geometry, grains an ygic iinsights are discussed in it
-> 2 branches krsna (black) and kukla (white)

**Sama veda**
-> sama indivates "to please, pacify or satisfy"
-> focus on musical chanting (singing of rgveda mantras)
-> melodic rendering of hymns
-> highlights the role of chanting in worship
-> 3 branches 
1. Kauthuma
2. Ranayaniya
3. jaiminiya
-> 2 parts
   Purvarcikam
   Utrararcikam
-> consists 1875 mantras

**atharv veda**
-> means one which brings wellnes, seen by sage Atharvan
-> priest is known as Brahman
-> monitoring of the vedic rituals
-> atharva is associated with everyday life and practices
-> focusis on everyday human concerns
