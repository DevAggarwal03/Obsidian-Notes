
## **Processing Crime and Incident Scenes**
**Identifying Digital Evidence:**
- General Tasks investigators perform when working with dig evidence
	1. identify digital info
	2. Collect, preserve and doc evidence
	3. Analyze, identify and organize evidence (each evidence is divided into diff forensics)
	4. Rebuild a situation to verfiy the results
- Any ditiatal evidence process is done systematically

**Imp points to be noted by investigators while presenting evidence in court**
- consistent practices for work verification
- Seeing the state rules (IT-Act etc)
- Be updated on current affairs
- any evidence present for criminal case can be used for civil case.
- any digital evidence in India falls under **documentary evidence**
- Digital evidence can be changed easyiliy, so verification is needed
- Another concern **Hearsay** (Second hand or indirect evidence) (avoided in courts)
