2. Chebysheff's theorem:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{The proportion of data within } &\ k \text{ standard deviations } (\mu - k\sigma \text{ to } \mu + k\sigma) \text{ is} \\
1 - \frac{1}{k}, \quad \text{for } k > 1
\end{aligned}
$$
