#### If Population is Normal
- The sample are also Normal distribution with 
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mu &\to \mu_p \text{ ( } \mu_p = \text{mean of population )} \\  
\sigma &\to \frac{\sigma_p}{\sqrt{n}} \text{ ( } \sigma_p = \text{mean of population, n = sample size )} \\  
\end{aligned}
$$
#### Properties if population is Normal 
1. Same mean (population and samples ka)
2. If n increases, standard deviation decreases
