- let X1, X2 .... Xn be different samples of a population
- Then Sample variance is:
$$
\begin{aligned}
S^2 &\to \frac{1}{n-1}\sum_{i=1}^{n}{(X_i - \bar{X})^2}
\end{aligned}
$$
- Here **S** is Sample standard error/deviation
