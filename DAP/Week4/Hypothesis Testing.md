-  This is used to determine to go with or against the statement about the population parameter (say mu or sigma)

#### Types of Hypotesis:
$$
\begin{aligned}
H_0 &\to \text{Null Hypothesis} \\
H_a &\to \text{Alternate Hypothesis}
\end{aligned}
$$
**Null Hypothesis**
- It denotes a tentative hypothesis about a population Parameter (always look from an optimistic perspective)

**Alternative Hypothesis**
- Opposite of what is stated in Null Hypothesis (from a pessimistic perspective)

**Hypothesis Testing (in short)**
- This procedure uses data to test the two competing statements H0 and Ha.
