- Most of the things depends on the **Alpha** (this is the **Level of Significance**)
- To check if the test should be right tile or left tile use the **Ha** (Alternative Hypothesis) 
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mu < \mu_o \text{ (Left Tile Testing)} \\
\mu > \mu_o \text{ (Right Tile Testing)}
\end{aligned}
$$
